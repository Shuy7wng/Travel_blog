🎓 Day 5 – Coimbra’s Culture and the Road to Porto (19 September)

📚 Morning – Discovering Coimbra

Start your day by exploring Coimbra, one of the oldest university cities in Europe, filled with history and charm.

  • Visit the prestigious University of Coimbra, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Admire the stunning Joanina Library, full of ancient books and ornate woodwork
  • Stop by the Old Cathedral (Sé Velha) for a glimpse into Romanesque architecture
  • Walk through the historic streets and enjoy views over the Mondego River

🚗 Afternoon – Journey to Porto

After lunch in Coimbra, head north to Porto (around 1.5 hours by car). Enjoy the drive through Portugal’s interior landscapes.

  • Check in at your hotel in Porto
  • Relax and take a short evening walk in the nearby area (Ribeira, if you're staying central)

🍽️ Evening – First Taste of Porto

Have dinner at a traditional restaurant by the river or try some local specialties like francesinha or bacalhau.
