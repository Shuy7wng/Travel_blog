✈️ Day 1 – Milan → Lisbon (15 September)

  • Morning: Direct flight from Milan to Lisbon (approx. 2 hours)
  • Arrival and hotel check-in
  • Afternoon: Walk through Lisbon's historic neighborhoods:
    • Baixa – the elegant downtown area
    • Chiado – lively and full of cafés, bookstores, and culture
    • Alfama – narrow streets, azulejos, and local charm
  • Evening: Traditional Portuguese dinner with live Fado music
