🍷 Day 6 – Exploring the Heart of Porto (20 September)

🏙️ Morning – Porto’s Historic Center

Start your day in the vibrant Ribeira district, along the Douro River.

  • Walk through colorful alleyways and riverside cafés
  • Cross the iconic Dom Luís I Bridge for stunning views
  • Visit the Clérigos Tower for a panoramic city view

📚 Afternoon – Bookstores, Churches and Wine

  • Stop by the magical Lello Bookstore, one of the most beautiful in the world
  • Admire the azulejos at São Bento Station and local churches
  • Take a Douro river cruise or visit a wine cellar for a Port tasting

🌉 Evening – Dinner with a View

End your day with a relaxing dinner by the river, under the lights of the bridge and city skyline.
