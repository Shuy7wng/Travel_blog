🌊 Day 4 – From Lisbon to Coimbra via Óbidos and Nazaré (18 September)

🚗 Morning – Óbidos

Leave Lisbon early and drive (or join a guided tour) to the charming village of Óbidos, a beautifully preserved medieval town surrounded by ancient walls.

  • Walk along the castle walls for panoramic views
  • Stroll through narrow streets filled with whitewashed houses and bougainvillea
  • Don't miss a taste of Ginjinha served in a chocolate cup

🌊 Afternoon – Nazaré

Continue north and arrive in Nazaré, a traditional fishing town famous for its gigantic waves.

  • Take the funicular up to Sítio for breathtaking ocean views
  • Explore the lighthouse and the wave-viewing points
  • Relax and enjoy the beach or seaside cafés

🏙️ Evening – Arrival in Coimbra

After sunset in Nazaré, head towards Coimbra (about 1 hour by car) to check into your hotel and rest for the next day's visit to the historic university town.
